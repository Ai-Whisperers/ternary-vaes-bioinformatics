# The Evolution and Emergence of RNA Viruses

**Author:** Edward C. Holmes
**Year:** 2009
**Type:** Book / Monograph
**Publisher:** Oxford University Press
**Link:** [Google Books / Publisher](https://global.oup.com/academic/product/the-evolution-and-emergence-of-rna-viruses-9780199211135)

## Abstract (Book Summary)

This book provides a comprehensive overview of the patterns and processes of evolutionary change in RNA viruses. It covers origins, mechanisms of evolution, viral quasispecies, comparative genomics, and molecular epidemiology. It includes case studies on influenza, HIV, dengue fever, and rabies, serving as a definitive text for understanding viral evolution.

## Key Concepts

- RNA Virus Quasispecies
- Viral Phylodynamics
- Zoonotic Emergence
- Comparative Genomics
