**Author:** Vossenaar, E.R., et al.
**Year:** 2009
**Journal:** Autoimmunity Reviews

## Abstract (Summary)

Comprehensive review of PADI4's physiological roles and its dysregulation in RA. Discusses the generation of ACPA-specific substrates and the varying susceptibility of _PADI4_ alleles across different ethnic populations.
