## 2. Focus Areas

- **Virology & Arboviruses:** Epidemiology, molecular characterization, and genomic surveillance of Dengue, Zika, and Chikungunya.
- **Diagnostic Technologies:** Development of local, low-cost diagnostic tests (ELISA, molecular assays) for infectious diseases (Chagas, Toxoplasmosis, Arboviruses).
- **Genomic Sequencing:** Implementation of MinION sequencing for SARS-CoV-2 and Arboviruses in Paraguay.
- **Biotechnology:** Recombinant protein production for diagnostics.

## 3. Key Research Projects

- **TUNeS-SR:** Tunable Nanomechanical Sensor with Specimen Recovery for continuous, real-time dengue virus detection (NIH 1 R01 AI175553-01).
- **RNAES:** Extraction and Storage of RNA at ambient temperature (NIH 1R01 AI173511-01).
- **Arboviral Genomic Surveillance:** Detection and characterization of Flavivirus and Alphavirus infections (NIH 1R21 AI46443-01).
- **Mobile Lab Unit:** Development of mobile laboratory capabilities for remote areas (Conacyt).
