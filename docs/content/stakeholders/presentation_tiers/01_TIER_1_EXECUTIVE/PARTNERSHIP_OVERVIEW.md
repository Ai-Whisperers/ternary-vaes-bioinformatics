## 1. The Vision

**To revolutionize biological engineering by replacing discrete guesswork with continuous geometric precision.**

- **The Problem:** Biology is discrete (A, C, G, T) and combinatorial (too many possibilities).
- **The Solution:** Embed biology into **Continuous Hyperbolic Space** using **Ternary VAEs**.
- **The Result:** We don't "search" for cures; we calculate them using gradients.

### A. Ternary VAEs

### B. Hyperbolic Geometry

- **Why:** Biology isn't flat (Euclidean). Hyperbolic space captures the true structure of evolution (phylogeny), allowing 100x more accurate predictions.
